This is a game based on the Red Riding Hood. The challenge is to kill all the black wolves and survive.

Game rules are simple. You must only kill black wolf. If you kill white wolf, your health point will go down by one.
If your health point becomes zero, then you lose.

Tapping on left side of screen will make Red Riding Hood jump. Tapping on right side will emit weapons. 
Moving the device on left or right side will make Red Riding Hood walk on the side of tilt. Thus, this game
uses accelerometer.

In short, to kill the black wolf, tap on right side of screen. 
To avoid being hurt by black wolf, tap on left side to jump.
To move, tilt the device on left or right side.
